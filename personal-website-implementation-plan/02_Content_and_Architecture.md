# Content and Architecture: Personal Website

## 1. Sitemap Structure (Sidebar)
1.  **About** (Home/Bio)
2.  **Experiences** (Work History)
3.  **Fieldnotes** (Writing/Essays)
4.  **My Philosophy** (Core Beliefs)
5.  **Photos** (Visual Diary)

## 2. Page-by-Page Breakdown

### 1. Home/About Page: "The Profile"
*   **Layout:** Two-column (Sidebar Left, Content Right).
*   **Header:** Huge Name "Name Surname" + Social Icons (Right aligned) + Theme Toggle.
*   **Bio:** "I'm a builder..." with **colored highlights** on key terms (Build Myanmar, TalentOS).
*   **"Cool things I've done":** A bulleted list of high-impact achievements.
*   **Filterable Grid:** Tabs [Everything | Projects | Communities].
    *   *Cards:* Minimalist cards showing projects like "Build Myanmar", "TalentOS".

### 2. Global Features
*   **Command+K:** A modal search bar giving quick access to any page or essay.
*   **Sidebar:** Sticky on desktop, hamburger on mobile. Lowercase, minimal font.

### 2. About Page: "The Journey"
*   **Narrative Flow:**
    *   **Origin:** Background, Buddhist philosophy influence.
    *   **The Mission:** Why rebuilding Myanmar matters. The intersection of ethics and technology.
    *   **The Approach:** How you think (Systems thinking, long-termism).
*   **Visuals:** A high-quality B&W portrait. candid shot of speaking or working.

### 3. Work Page: "The Ecosystem"
*   **Section A: TalentOS (Current Focus)**
    *   *Focus:* Global skill/identity platform. Proof of work. Fair opportunity.
    *   *Action:* Link to TalentOS site.
*   **Section B: Build Myanmar (The Foundation)**
    *   *Focus:* The ecosystem, the media, the nation-building initiatives.
    *   *Metrics:* 5k members, physical spaces, podcasts.
*   **Section C: Projects/Initiatives**
    *   Smaller list of other specific initiatives (Hackathons, content series).

### 4. Writing Page: "The Philosophy"
*   **Layout:** Clean, focused reading list.
*   **Categories:** "Philosophy", "Building", "Myanmar", "Systems".
*   **Presentation:** Title, Date, Reading Time, Short excerpt. Minimal list view.
*   **Individual Article View:** Focus Mode. Single column text, serif font for readability, high contrast.

### 5. Contact Page
*   **Purpose:** Low friction way to connect.
*   **Content:**
    *   "Where to find me" (Social links).
    *   "Collaborate" (Email or specific contact form for business).
    *   "Join the Community" (Links to Build Myanmar/TalentOS communities).

## 3. Story Flow (Top to Bottom)
*   **Top:** Attract with high-level vision and synthesis of identity.
*   **Middle:** Prove the vision with tangible work (TalentOS) and deep thought (Writing).
*   **Bottom:** Invite connection and action.
